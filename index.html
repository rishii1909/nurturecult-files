Application of technology

Technology is all around us, from smartphones and computers to self driven cars and industrial robots. Starting its journey from the Industrial revolution in 1760s, technology has advanced in leaps and bounds and everything is available to us at our doorstep. We certainly owe a lot to our ancestors for leaving us such a legacy.

Technology is the most fascinating and captivating term we have ever come across, yet understanding its applications in the real world can engross our minds forever. As enthralling as the words "Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain" sound, truly recognizing their uses in our day to day lives can get scratchy. And believe us, their application in making things work is far, quite far then we can possibly think.

In our schools, we seldom are introduced to topics like the working of internet, how applications survive in the market or even how to develop a working logic for any 'functionality'. Today, data is the new oil and if used to full potential, data can optimize and enhance everyone's style of living. Such is the importance of technology, that we cannot imagine our lives without it.

Did you know that over 90% of the engineering students end up having a job in coding profile? You will be quite surprised to know that half the students of this category have a pretty inadequate amount of knowledge of how to proceed in such a field and follow the herd behaviour in making not well-thought-out decisions concerning their career.

For people like us, who are still figuring out what is the best possible path to success and getting quirky in making life decisions, this a perfect place to recognize and learn the true nature of tech. Come and learn the basics of technology, and explore a whole new realm with us.

The aim of this course is to give a brief introduction to the application of the most common forms of Technology in the real world, covering a wide spectrum of our day to day life use cases, to its contribution in big Multi National Companies

With the help of this course, the student will be able to:

-- Understand the working concept of Internet, Software Development
-- Understand what topics are needed to be covered to develop proficiency in Coding
-- Understand the economics of an application and how it survives in the competent world
-- Understand and implement the concepts of Machine Learning, Big Data
-- Relate to Cloud Computing, Internet of Things, Blockchain, 3-D printing, and other smart techniques

People might think diving into such a vast domain might get hectic and tiresome, but we assure you all of it is worth for the insights you will gain as this course ends.

Family

Has anyone ever taught you what to order in a cafe or restaurant? Or anything about the table etiquettes? Moving forward in life you will need to know how to behave and how your body language should be because society is judgemental and that is a fact. We judge people’s skills by how they talk or present themselves. No matter how petty this sounds, it is a fact.

So, how to create your first impression? Why do you hesitate while going for a date? Why do you feel nervous while delivering a presentation? And how shall the dressing be for any specific kind of event? We can argue that we will learn this with experience but if it matters so much why not know it at the right time. Being able to carry yourself elegantly is an art It expresses one’s personality and people acknowledge your presence.

Apart from that, there are other very basic life skills that define your everyday decisions. How to act and perform wisely? How to bargain with shopkeeper? How to tackle with your anger? How to manage your personal life with academics and corporate work? These are things that are not taught in a conventional education system but are highly needed.

We have tried to pack all these life hacks in this week-long workshop. We can guarantee you that you will see a change in yourself and you will be able to explore the best version of yourself.

Finance

Have you seen The Big Short or The Wolf of the Wall Street or for that matter Hritik Roshan in Zindagi Na Milegi Dobaara? It sounds cool right to call yourself an investor and keep piling up money? Well, let me tell you for a fact that Warren Buffet, one of the greatest investors of our time, started investing in stocks at the age of 11. Yes, you heard it right, 11! I remember watching Doraemon and Chhota Bheem at that age.

Frankly speaking, investing isn’t about complex statistics or probabilistic models. It can start with a simple understanding of current news revolving around the company and their promoter group. You slowly develop a clear understanding of the company’s technical charts and fundamental valuation to grow into a decent investor. It’s all about the experience, patience and instincts.

How many schools teach us about the stock market or how to write a cheque or how to withdraw money from the bank. If we can dare to understand trigonometry at that age then we can surely give this a shot. In unprecedented times like this, investing is the best option to store and grow money and have a guaranteed income without a job. We need this!

You are a school kid or a college-going lad or a middle-age person re-thinking your life choices, this is the place where it all starts. Come and learn the basics of the stock market and earn a ton of money while having fun in this lockdown. Yes, ask the investors, lockdown can be fun!

The topics that will be covered in this 1-week workshop are as follows:

  1. Micro and Macro Economics
  2. Financial Markets and Investment
  3. Corporate Finance
  4. Stress Management and Happiness
  5. Portfolio Management Project

All of this might sound gibberish right now but let me tell you that after this 1 week you will have command over your money and have a general awareness of day-to-day economics.

You will also be given a real-time project that will boost your confidence and you will also gain experience in being an actual investor. Top project performer will be receiving exciting prizes.
